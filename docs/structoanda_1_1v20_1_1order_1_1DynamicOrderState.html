oanda::v20::pricing_common::PriceValue oanda::v20::order::DynamicOrderState::triggerDistance

The distance between the Trailing Stop Loss Order’s trailingStopValue and the current Market Price.

This represents the distance (in price units) of the Order from a triggering price. If the distance could not be determined, this value will not be set.
